Where it all happens
who's behind it

Started by four Austin students. Going worldwide.

We're Atharv, Jayden, Max, and Gaurav - four incoming freshmen from Austin. We kept noticing the same thing around school: kids without the basics. A pencil. A notebook. A backpack. Small stuff that's easy to overlook until it's the reason a kid falls behind.

So we started The Pencil Project. Right now it's just the four of us - collecting supplies, running small drives, and dropping them off at schools and libraries near us. We're starting in Austin, but the goal is bigger: no kid should fall behind over a notebook, anywhere in the world. We're still figuring a lot out, but we mean it.

- The Pencil Project Team

why it matters

A pencil is a small thing.
Going without one isn't.

When a kid has their own supplies, school stops being about what they're missing and starts being about what they can do.

Their own notebook and pencils means walking in ready to learn, not stuck borrowing or sitting out.
