what we host

From collection to classroom.

Every drive follows the same simple path - here's how it goes.

01

Collect

Drop-off drives where neighbors donate pencils, notebooks, books, and backpacks.

02

Raise

Fundraisers that let us buy supplies in bulk and grow the scholarship fund.

03

Pack

Hands-on volunteer days where we sort and pack everything for delivery.

04

Deliver

The best part - dropping supplies at the classrooms and libraries that asked.
