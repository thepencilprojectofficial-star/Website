Meet the team.

The Pencil Project runs on students from right here in Austin who decided to do something instead of wait.

founders

The two who started it.

Both incoming high school freshmen, both from Austin.

AG

Atharv Gupta

Founder · incoming freshman

Started The Pencil Project on a simple idea: no Austin student should fall behind because they couldn't afford a notebook - and one day, no student anywhere. He leads the drives and keeps the mission moving.

JM

Jayden Mailhot

Marketing Lead · incoming freshman

Jumped in early as co-founder and helps run fundraising, outreach, and the day-to-day of getting supplies where they need to go all around the world.
